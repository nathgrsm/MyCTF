Esports Analytics and Data
Esports analytics and esports data are the key elements of what we focus the most on. Not only Esports Charts collects and compiles data, but also uses the stats to conduct research of different scale: from small studies to determining global trends of the entire industry.
Esports Popularity
One of our goals is to measure the popularity of esports. On a daily basis we take into account a wide variety of viewership metrics. Each of them provides a different perspective that helps us to see the full picture of the market.
Esports Viewership and Statistics
Esports audience varies greatly depending on a discipline, tournament or even season. We analyze esports viewership numbers to understand the current state of affairs in the industry.
